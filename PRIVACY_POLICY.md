# Privacy Policy — Meditation Timer

**Effective date:** April 24, 2026  
**Developer contact:** AppHux@gmail.com

---

## Summary

Meditation Timer is a fully offline app. **No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or shared with any third party — ever.** All data lives only on your device and is under your full control.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Meditation Timer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

All data — including your meditation sessions, mood logs, practice settings, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device in private app storage. Nothing is sent to external servers, third-party analytics platforms, or any other service.

---

## 2. Local Data Storage

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- Meditation session history (date, duration, mood ratings)
- Practice configurations you create
- App preferences and settings (timer durations, reminder times, theme choice)
- Background music file paths you select from your device

This data is held in the app's private SharedPreferences storage and local database.

---

## 3. Data Retention

Because all data is stored locally on your device, **you are in full control of how long it is kept.**

- Data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the app installed.
- No data is ever sent to, or retained by, any external server or the developer.

---

## 4. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time using the following methods:

1. **Delete individual sessions** — Open the Sessions screen, swipe or long-press a session, and delete it.
2. **Clear all app data** — Go to your device's **Settings → Apps → Meditation Timer → Storage → Clear Data**. This permanently removes all sessions, practices, preferences, and settings.
3. **Uninstall the app** — Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device automatically.

There is no account or server-side data to request deletion of, because we never collect or store anything off your device.

---

## 5. Storage & File Access

If you choose to use background music, the app requests access to your device's audio files:

- **Android 13+:** `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` permission
- **Android 12 and below:** `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission (scoped to audio)

This access is used **solely** to read music files you explicitly select. The app does not scan, index, or upload your files.

---

## 6. Notifications

The app may request the `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` permission (Android 13+) to deliver a daily meditation reminder if you enable that feature in Settings.

Notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device — no network calls are made.

---

## 7. Alarms & Reminders

The app uses the `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` / `USE_EXACT_ALARM` permission to deliver practice reminders at the times you set. This is used solely to trigger local notifications; no data is transmitted.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

The app does **not** use:

- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics services (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel)
- Crash-reporting services (e.g. Crashlytics)
- Any third-party library that transmits data off your device

There are **no trackers, no analytics, and no ads.**

---

## 9. Data Export & Import

The Sessions screen provides an optional CSV export/import feature. Exported files are saved to a location you choose on your device and are **never uploaded automatically**. You are in full control of any data you export.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Because all data stays on-device, there is no mechanism by which children's data could be collected or shared.

---

## 11. Your Rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored off your device, traditional data-subject rights (access, portability, correction, erasure) are satisfied entirely through the on-device deletion methods described in **Section 4** above. If you have any questions, contact us at **AppHux@gmail.com** and we will respond within 30 days.
